What We Can Help With

支持范围

  • Account registration, login, verification code, password reset, and profile issues.
  • Practice records, favorites, mistake review, learning reports, and data sync issues.
  • Question quality feedback, app bugs, display issues, and usability suggestions.
  • Account deletion, data access, correction, or other privacy-related requests.
  • 账号注册、登录、验证码、密码重置和个人资料问题。
  • 刷题记录、收藏夹、错题复习、学习报告和数据同步问题。
  • 题目质量反馈、应用异常、显示问题和使用体验建议。

Information to Include

反馈时请提供

  • Your account email or phone number used in the app.
  • Device model, iOS/iPadOS version, and app version.
  • The page or feature where the issue happened.
  • Time of occurrence, screenshots, and steps to reproduce the issue if available.
  • 你在 App 内使用的账号邮箱或手机号。
  • 设备型号、iOS/iPadOS 系统版本和 App 版本。
  • 问题发生的页面或功能名称。
  • 问题发生时间、截图，以及可复现步骤。
